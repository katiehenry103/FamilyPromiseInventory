Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new location? ▾
Tap the location name in the top header bar, or tap Switch Location on the Dashboard. At the bottom of the dropdown, tap + Add New Location. Enter a name and choose a color, then tap Add Location. You will be switched to that location automatically.
How do I scan an item's barcode? ▾
Go to the Add Item tab. Tap Start Scanner — your camera will open. Point it at the barcode on the product. The app will automatically read the barcode and look up the product name. If scanning doesn't work, you can type the barcode number in the manual entry box below the scanner, or just type the item name directly.
How do I add an item manually without scanning? ▾
Go to the Add Item tab. Skip the scanner and type the item name directly into the Item Name field on the right. Fill in the category, quantity, unit, best-by date, and storage area, then tap Save to Inventory.
How do I adjust the quantity of an item? ▾
Go to the Inventory tab. Find the item and tap the − or + buttons next to the quantity, or tap Adjust. Enter how much to add or remove, choose Incoming or Outgoing, add an optional note, and tap Save Change. If the quantity reaches zero, the item is automatically removed from inventory.
How do I switch between locations? ▾
Tap the location name in the purple header bar at the top, or tap Switch Location on the Dashboard. A dropdown will appear showing all your locations. Tap any location to switch to it. Each location has its own separate inventory.
How do I delete a location? ▾
Open the location switcher by tapping the location name in the header. Each location has a small ✕ button on the right side. Tap it and confirm to delete the location and all of its inventory items. This cannot be undone, so be careful.
What does the Dashboard show? ▾
The Dashboard shows a summary of the current location — total number of items, number of categories, how many items are expiring within 7 days, and how many are already expired. Items that need attention are listed at the bottom so you can act on them quickly.
What do Fresh, Expiring Soon, and Expired mean? ▾
These are freshness statuses based on the best-by date you entered. Fresh means more than 7 days until expiry. Expiring Soon means 7 days or less remaining. Expired means the best-by date has already passed. Items that are expiring soon or expired show up on the Dashboard under Needs Attention.
Will my data be saved if I close the app? ▾
Yes. All data is saved to a secure cloud database in real time. You can close the app, switch devices, or have multiple volunteers use it at the same time — everyone will always see the same up-to-date inventory.
How do I export the inventory? ▾
Go to the Inventory tab and tap Export CSV. This downloads a spreadsheet file of the current location's inventory that can be opened in Excel or Google Sheets.
How do I log in and what are the passwords? ▾
Contact Katherine or Anna at katiehenry103@gmail.com to get your login credentials. Do not share your password with anyone outside your organization.
